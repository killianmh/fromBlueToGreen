About Us

Ellie founded From Blue to Green in 2019 after seeing the massive amounts of waste that our healthcare facilities generate. Having a decade of volunteering and non-profit experience, she saw an opportunity and went to work. She started collecting used blue wrap and organizing volunteers in the community to sew totes and sleeping bags. The movement has been growing ever since!
